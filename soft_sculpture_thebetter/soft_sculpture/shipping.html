How Cash on Delivery Works

1

Place Your Order

Browse our collection and add items to your cart. Fill in your delivery details at checkout.

2

Confirm Your Order

Review your order details and confirm. We'll send you a confirmation email with your order number.

3

We Prepare Your Order

We carefully package your items and prepare them for shipping. This usually takes 1-2 business days.

4

Delivery & Payment

Our delivery personnel will bring your order to your address. Pay the total amount directly to them in cash.

5

Enjoy Your Purchase

Unbox your items and enjoy your beautiful fuzzy wire sculptures! Need support? We're here to help.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

A: Contact us immediately if you need to change your address. We can assist if the order hasn't been shipped yet.

Q: What happens if I'm not at home when the delivery arrives?

A: Our delivery personnel will contact you. You can arrange for a re-delivery or have someone receive the order on your behalf.

Q: Are there any additional fees besides the shipping fee?

A: No. The price shown includes the product cost and shipping fee. No hidden charges when you pay to the delivery personnel.

Q: What if my order is damaged upon delivery?

A: Document the damage with photos and contact us immediately. We'll process a replacement or refund for you.

Q: How do I track my order?

A: You'll receive an order confirmation email with tracking information. You can also login to your account to check order status.
