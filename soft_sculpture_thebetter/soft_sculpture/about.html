Our Story

Soft Sculpture started as a passion project from our couple-run creative team. We began by transforming simple fuzzy wires into beautiful, unique art pieces that bring joy and color to people's lives.

Each design is carefully shaped with precision and imagination, resulting in charming sculptures that range from cute characters to decorative items perfect for gifts, souvenirs, and personal collections.

What started as a small hobby has evolved into a thriving business dedicated to spreading creativity and bringing handmade art to customers across the nation.
